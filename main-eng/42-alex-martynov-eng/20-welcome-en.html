Welcome to "Austrian School of Economics in Ukrainian"

We are proud to introduce you to the first austro-libertarian site in Ukraine. Ukraine is a country with both strong statist inclinations and a huge hatred for regime that has established itself since the collapse of USSR. We hope our site will become a foundation for spreading libertarian ideas in this country (while our country still exists).

Besides translations of Austrian economic writing which, as the title suggests, will be in Ukrainian, there are also going to be blogs with comments on state of affairs in Ukraine through austro-libertarian perspective.

Feel free to leave comments and questions.
